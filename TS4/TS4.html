Margherita

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A timeless Italian classic, the Margherita pizza is a simple yet delicious dish that highlights the freshest ingredients. Featuring a thin, crispy crust baked to perfection, it's topped with a rich and tangy tomato sauce made from sun-ripened San Marzano tomatoes, creamy and melted mozzarella cheese, and fragrant fresh basil leaves. The combination of flavors creates a perfect balance between savory, cheesy, and herby notes, making it a go-to choice for pizza lovers who appreciate authentic flavors. Finished with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, this pizza is a must-try for those who love classic Italian cuisine.

Cheeseburger

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A juicy and mouthwatering cheeseburger made with a perfectly seasoned, flame-grilled beef patty, topped with melted cheddar cheese, fresh lettuce, crisp pickles, thick tomato slices, and a hint of tangy burger sauce. All these ingredients are sandwiched between a soft, toasted sesame seed bun that enhances the overall texture and taste. Every bite delivers a rich and hearty experience, packed with umami flavors from the seared meat, the creaminess of the cheese, and the fresh crunch of the veggies. It's a classic American favorite that never fails to satisfy cravings!

Garlic Parmesan Pasta

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This creamy and flavorful pasta dish is a true comfort food with a luxurious taste. The pasta is perfectly cooked al dente and tossed in a rich, velvety garlic butter sauce infused with slow-roasted garlic and premium Parmesan cheese. The cheese melts into the sauce, creating a smooth and creamy coating that clings to each strand of spaghetti. A touch of black pepper and parsley adds a mild kick and fresh contrast, making every bite indulgent. For extra depth, the dish is finished with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan and red pepper flakes, making it irresistibly delicious.

Vanilla Milkshake

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A smooth and creamy vanilla milkshake that is both refreshing and indulgent. Made with premium vanilla bean ice cream, fresh whole milk, and a touch of whipped cream, this shake delivers an authentic and rich vanilla flavor in every sip. Topped with fluffy whipped cream, rainbow sprinkles, and a maraschino cherry, it's both a treat for the eyes and the taste buds. This milkshake is thick, satisfying, and perfectly balanced between sweet and creamy, making it an ideal dessert or a refreshing companion to any meal.
